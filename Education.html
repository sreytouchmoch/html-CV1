* Importance Of Education For Country

A sound educational system works for the good of the country. A country mainly is judge by its economy and education system. A nation is growing day by day just because of its technology and education.

(i) Choose Good Leader: Educated people understand the difference between a good and an evil leader for their country to lead. Educated people know why to vote for a party to bring a positive resolution to the development of the country. (ii) Prevent Poverty: Due to the lack of education, people are often unemployed that shows the low level of economic status of a nation. Pathetic plight of a country obstructs its progress and development. Thus, education is must to boost economic growth and increase income. (iii) Health Standard: Education also promotes the health status of a country. A quality education gives many pharmacists and doctors to a nation. Therefore, the citizens of a nation live healthy and happy. (iv) Provides Services: A country can be serviceable on behalf of education. The blend of ideas and techniques given by educated people brings invention and industrial development. And also, educated people can work with them. As a result, the country is known as more powerful and achieve significant fame in advancement.
